    FAQ

    The engine triggers an automatic download for each converted file directly into your browser's default download folder.
    SVG is a vector format, meaning you can scale your pixel art to any size (like 4K or billboards) without losing quality.
    We recommend images up to 256x256. Larger files might cause the browser tab to hang due to heavy geometric calculations.
    Once the engine is loaded, GLORP runs entirely on your hardware. You can disconnect from the internet and keep converting.
    The core "Contour Meshing" algorithm was developed and optimized by Harry Tsang for maximum efficiency.
